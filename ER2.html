About the producer

Hidetaka Miyazaki (宮崎 英高, Miyazaki Hidetaka, born c. 1974–1975) is a Japanese video game director, designer, writer, and executive of FromSoftware. He originally joined the company in 2004 as a game designer for the Armored Core series and before becoming better known for creating the Souls series. He was promoted to company president in 2014 and also serves as its representative director. Other notable games he has directed include Bloodborne, Sekiro, and Elden Ring.

George R. R. Martin

George Raymond Richard Martin[1] (born George Raymond Martin; September 20, 1948),[2] also known as GRRM,[3] is an American novelist, screenwriter, television producer and short story writer. He is the author of the series of epic fantasy novels A Song of Ice and Fire, which were adapted into the Emmy Award-winning HBO series Game of Thrones (2011–2019). He also helped create the Wild Cards anthology series, and contributed worldbuilding for the 2022 video game Elden Ring.
